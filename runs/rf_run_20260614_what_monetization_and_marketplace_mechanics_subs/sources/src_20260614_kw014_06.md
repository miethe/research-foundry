# Source Card: Payments Policy | Ribblr

## Summary
Ribblr's official Payments Policy (dated 6 December 2024) sets out the fee structure for its crochet/knitting pattern marketplace. Pattern sale fees range from FREE to 4% per sale (with a vendor-set tier and a $elFee path to 0%), subject to minimum sale fees of 25¢ for patterns and 50¢ for finished makes, plus a 50¢ designer royalty on finished-make sales. Core creator tooling — listing, the Ribbuild pattern builder, translation, smart sizing, and media uploads — is offered free, while tips incur a 5% service fee and Stripe processing fees are charged separately.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) Pattern sale fee ranges from FREE to 4% per sale, standard tier starting at 4%.
- (ev_002) Minimum sale fee is 25¢ for patterns and 50¢ for finished makes.
- (ev_003) A 50¢ designer royalty is collected on finished-make sales and paid to the original pattern's designer.
- (ev_004) The $elFee program can reduce a seller's pattern sale fee to nil (0%).
- (ev_005) Tips carry a 5% tip service fee; Stripe payment processing fees are charged separately on top.
- (ev_006) Listing, Ribbuild, US/UK translation, smart sizing, unit conversion, and media uploads are all free.

## Limitations
- Vendor self-reported policy page; fee tiers and amounts may change without notice, and the exact Stripe processing rate is not stated (only that Stripe "may charge" a fee).
