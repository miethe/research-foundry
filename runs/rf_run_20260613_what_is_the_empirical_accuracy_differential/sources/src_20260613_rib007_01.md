# Source Card: Introducing Claude Haiku 4.5

## Summary
Anthropic's official announcement (October 15, 2025) introduces Claude Haiku 4.5, a small/fast model priced at $1/$5 per million input/output tokens. The post frames Haiku 4.5 as delivering near-frontier coding quality — comparable to Sonnet 4 from five months earlier — at roughly one-third the cost and 2x+ the speed, with partner Augment reporting 90% of Sonnet 4.5's agentic-coding performance. It also reports favorable safety results, with a statistically significantly lower rate of misaligned behaviors than both Sonnet 4.5 and Opus 4.1.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) Released Oct 15, 2025 at $1/MTok input and $5/MTok output.
- (ev_002) Matches Sonnet 4 coding performance at one-third the cost and 2x+ the speed.
- (ev_003) Augment eval: 90% of Sonnet 4.5's agentic-coding performance.
- (ev_004) Runs up to 4-5x faster than Sonnet 4.5 at a fraction of the cost.
- (ev_005) Surpasses Sonnet 4 on some tasks, such as computer use.
- (ev_006) Statistically significantly lower misalignment rate than Sonnet 4.5 and Opus 4.1.

## Limitations
- This is a first-party vendor product announcement (marketing framing); the headline coding/speed numbers are vendor- or partner-reported (Augment), not independently audited. The 90% and 4-5x figures derive from Augment's evaluation/quotes rather than a neutral third-party benchmark.
