# Source Card: Announcing Edison Scientific

## Summary
A first-party FutureHouse announcement (Nov 5, 2025) declaring the launch of Edison Scientific, a for-profit commercial spinout that will develop and deploy FutureHouse's AI Scientist for commercial use, while the nonprofit FutureHouse continues foundational biology research. It explains the rationale (overwhelming demand and rate-limit requests since the May 2025 platform launch, including inbound interest from 6 of the top 10 pharma companies), the dual-leadership governance under Sam Rodriques and Andrew White with controls against self-dealing, and a commitment to a generous free tier alongside paid features for power users.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) FutureHouse is launching Edison Scientific, a commercial spinout to develop/deploy the AI Scientist for commercial applications.
- (ev_002) A portion of the FutureHouse team is moving to Edison; FutureHouse continues foundational biology research.
- (ev_003) Rodriques and White run both entities with stated controls against self-dealing and conflicts of interest.
- (ev_004) Edison commits to a generous free tier with paid features for power users needing higher rate limits.
- (ev_005) Demand since the May 2025 platform launch (rate-limit requests) motivated the spinout.
- (ev_006) Inbound interest came from VP/C-level executives at 6 of the top 10 pharma companies.
- (ev_007) Productization/payments/GTM/support are argued not to be the right use of philanthropic funding.

## Limitations
- The ~$70M funding raise and the Kosmos product details mentioned in the task brief are NOT stated in this announcement; they require separate sourcing.
- Migration URLs (docs/platform.edisonscientific.com) are not confirmed in this page text.
- Free-tier and governance commitments are forward-looking pledges, not independently verified outcomes; the source is self-promotional.
