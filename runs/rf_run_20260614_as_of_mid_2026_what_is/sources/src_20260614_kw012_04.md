# Source Card: Stitch Fiddle Premium pricing (official)

## Summary
Stitch Fiddle's official Premium pricing page documents a free/Premium tier split for its browser-based 2D grid charting tool (knitting, crochet, cross-stitch). Free is capped at 15 charts, 50 colors, and a 300x300 grid; Premium ($5.50/mo or $33.00/yr = $2.75/mo, billed as a 50% annual discount) raises limits to unlimited charts, 250 colors, and a 1,000x1,000 grid and unlocks image upload, collaboration, QR codes, vector/Word/Excel export, error checking, and written-instruction conversion. Payments are one-off with no auto-renewal, and the product is cross-device, browser-only 2D charting.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) Free tier caps usage at 15 charts, 50 colors/symbols per chart, and a 300x300 (90,000-stitch) grid.
- (ev_002) Premium raises limits to unlimited charts, 250 colors/symbols, and a 1,000x1,000 (1,000,000-stitch) grid.
- (ev_003) Premium is $5.50/month or $33.00/year ($2.75/month), framed as a 50% annual discount.
- (ev_004) Payments are one-off with no automatic renewal; subscriptions expire automatically.
- (ev_005) Premium-only features: image upload, collaboration, QR codes, vector/Word/Excel export, error checking, knitting-instructions conversion.
- (ev_006) Browser-based across Windows/Mac/Android/iOS/Linux/Chromebook with automatic cross-device sync.

## Limitations
- No publication/last-updated date is visible on the page, so pricing and feature lists are point-in-time (accessed 2026-06-14) and may change. As a vendor pricing page it is authoritative for plan structure but inherently promotional. The page describes 2D grid charting only — no 3D visualization or assembly guidance is offered, consistent with the 3D-preview whitespace.
