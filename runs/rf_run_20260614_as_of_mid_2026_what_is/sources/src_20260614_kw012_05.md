# Source Card: Pattern Keeper (official site)

## Summary
Pattern Keeper is a cross-stitch chart-tracking app developed by Åsa Falkenjack at Datadromeda AB. Its official site establishes pricing ($9.00 one-time in-app purchase for lifetime access, with a one-month all-features free trial), the core tracking feature set (continuous cross-page chart viewing, symbol search/highlight, multi-direction/diagonal stitch selection, progress tallies and per-color stitch counts), supported PDF-chart designers, and platform support (Android 4.1+; iOS only in early development with newsletter signup). The scope is tracking-only; it does not provide row-by-row 3D rendering, assembly, or amigurumi tooling.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) Pattern Keeper costs \$9.00 as a one-time in-app purchase granting permanent (use-forever) access.
- (ev_002) The app offers a one-month free trial that includes access to all features.
- (ev_003) Core features include continuous chart viewing across page breaks, symbol search/highlight, and stitch selection by clicking/dragging in any direction including diagonally.
- (ev_004) Progress tracking colors finished stitches with the thread color and shows stitches left per color.
- (ev_005) Works with PDF charts from Heaven and Earth Designs, Paine Free Crafts, Charting Creations, Artecy, and other compatible designers.
- (ev_006) Photographed paper patterns import but lose search and thread-number functionality.
- (ev_007) Android 4.1+ with Google Play Store required; 2GB+ RAM recommended; avoid Android 8.1.
- (ev_008) iOS version is in early development and not yet available; iOS newsletter signup offered.

## Limitations
- Self-reported vendor/marketing source: feature, pricing, and platform claims are the developer's own and are not independently verified.
- Scope is cross-stitch chart tracking only; the site documents no row-by-row 3D rendering, garment/amigurumi assembly, or non-counted-thread craft tooling.
- Published/copyright year recorded as 2026; specific feature-version dating is not given on the page.
