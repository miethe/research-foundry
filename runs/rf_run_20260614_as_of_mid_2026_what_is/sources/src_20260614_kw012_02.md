# Source Card: Upgrade and become a Ribblr+ member (official subscription page)

## Summary
Ribblr's official subscription page establishes the structure and pricing of its Ribblr+ membership: three monthly USD tiers (Plus $4.99, Gold $6.99, Platinum $9.99, each shown against a higher "listed" price), with a 30% discount for annual billing and a 14-day free trial that auto-renews on day 14. It enumerates the premium feature set (unlimited daily free patterns, boards, external/PDF pattern import, no ads, Focus Mode), tier-differentiated Gems allowances, and a cancel-anytime, pro-rata upgrade/downgrade policy.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) Three monthly USD tiers: Plus $4.99 (listed $6.99), Gold $6.99 (listed $9.99), Platinum $9.99 (listed $13.99).
- (ev_002) Annual billing saves 30% across all tiers.
- (ev_003) 14-day free trial precedes auto-renewal; charged on day 14.
- (ev_004) All tiers include unlimited daily free patterns, boards, external patterns, no ads, and Focus Mode.
- (ev_005) Gems allowance differs by tier: Plus = 100 (joining gift), Gold = 100/month, Platinum = 400/month.
- (ev_006) Membership enables adding external PDF patterns to the library.
- (ev_007) Cancel-anytime with pro-rata pricing on tier changes (upgrade pays only the difference).

## Limitations
- No publication date is shown on the page, so prices and terms may change without notice; this is vendor-authored marketing copy, not an independently audited source. The page does not describe a "Ribbuild PDF-to-ePattern conversion" feature, so that specific SOURCE key point is not asserted here. The page labels Plus's 100 Gems a "joining gift" (one-time) rather than a recurring monthly allowance, contrary to the SOURCE summary; the recurring monthly Gems wording applies to Gold (100/mo) and Platinum (400/mo).
