# Source Card: knitCompanion knitting & more (Apple App Store listing)

## Summary
The Apple App Store listing for knitCompanion (developer Create2Thrive Inc.) describes a multi-craft pattern tracker covering knitting, crochet, and cross-stitch. A free kCBasics tier offers row/progress tracking, per-project counters, iCloud sync, and Ravelry/Dropbox linking with PDF import; paid tiers (Setup + Essentials / Essentials) add Scribble annotation, Magic Markers, Intelligent Chart Recognition, customizable setup, and hands-free voice commands. The product scope is PDF/chart overlay tracking on Apple and Android platforms, not 3D structural visualization or guided assembly.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) Published by Create2Thrive Inc.; positioned as multi-craft (knit/crochet/cross-stitch) tracker.
- (ev_002) Free kCBasics tier tracks rows and along-the-row progress with per-project counters and iCloud sync.
- (ev_003) Free tier links to Ravelry and Dropbox for pattern import.
- (ev_004) Works with any pattern, including added PDFs, pitched for mystery knit-a-longs.
- (ev_005) Paid tiers add Magic Markers to count, highlight, and color stitches.
- (ev_006) Paid tiers add Intelligent Chart Recognition for faster chart setup.
- (ev_007) Paid tiers add Scribble annotation and hands-free voice commands.
- (ev_008) Runs on iPhone/iPad/Mac (16.4+/13.3+), rated 4+, Lifestyle category, version 3.0.20 (Dec 2025).

## Limitations
- Feature descriptions are first-party marketing copy from the developer, not independent verification.
- Pricing is not asserted: the live App Store listing showed in-app purchases at \$29.99, which conflicts with the run's prior key_points (~\$0.99/month or \$9.99/year) and a third-party review (Essentials \$9.99/yr). Subscription/IAP pricing is volatile and tier/region-dependent, so no specific dollar figure is treated as confirmed.
- Android availability is stated by the developer/store ecosystem; this card was sourced from the iOS App Store listing.
