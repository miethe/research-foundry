# Source Card: What is SelFee? — Ribblr Help Center

## Summary
Ribblr's official help-center article documents SelFee, a referral-driven seller fee-discount program on the Ribblr crochet/knitting marketplace. Designers earn points whenever someone uses their personal SelFee link; accumulated points convert to sale-fee discounts (up to 100% off), with a defined 10,000-point -> 10%-off conversion, a monthly point rollover, and a 1,000-point first-month welcome bonus. The program covers both pattern sales and finished "makes".

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) SelFee is a personal smart-fee mechanism giving sellers control over their sale fees.
- (ev_002) Sellers can earn up to 100% off their sale fee, eliminating the fee on pattern sales.
- (ev_003) Points are earned each time someone uses the seller's SelFee link.
- (ev_004) 10,000 points yields an instant 10% off plus 1,000 points carried into the next month.
- (ev_005) New users get a 1,000-point welcome bonus in their first month.
- (ev_006) SelFee applies to both patterns and finished makes.
