# Source Card: Ribblr - Crochet & Knitting (Apple App Store listing)

## Summary
The Apple App Store listing for Ribblr (v3.02, released April 13, 2025) establishes the app as a crochet/knitting/sewing platform centered on interactive ePatterns with line-by-line progress tracking, per-user custom-size views, and built-in video tutorials. It also documents the Ribbuild PDF-to-interactive-ePattern conversion tooling, a buy/sell pattern marketplace, broad Apple-ecosystem availability (iPhone, iPad, iPod touch, Mac M1+, Apple Vision), a 4.2/5 rating from 468 ratings, and a ~4 MB free download with in-app purchases. Notably, the listing advertises no 3D visualization or guided-assembly capability.

- (ev_005) Version 3.02 was released April 13 (2025); 4.2/5 from 468 ratings; ~4 MB download.
