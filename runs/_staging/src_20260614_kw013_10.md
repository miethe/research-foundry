# Source Card: DMCA Notice Requirements - Our House Rules | Etsy

## Summary
Etsy's published DMCA Notice Requirements page sets out the procedures and required elements for submitting copyright infringement notices and counter notices on the platform. It establishes the Etsy Reporting Portal (etsy.com/ipreporting) as the preferred channel with legal@etsy.com as a fallback, enumerates the six elements of a valid notice and the elements of a counter notice (including federal-court jurisdiction consent), and warns of misrepresentation liability and possible account termination for abusive filings. The page is Etsy's own legal policy and was last updated June 16, 2023.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) Etsy prefers reports via the Etsy Reporting Portal (etsy.com/ipreporting); email to legal@etsy.com is the fallback.
- (ev_002) A valid notice requires six specific elements, including the specific Etsy listing URL of infringing material.
- (ev_003) Misrepresenting that material is infringing may create liability for damages including costs and attorneys' fees.
- (ev_004) Fraudulent or abusive notices may result in account termination or other legal consequences.
- (ev_005) Counter notices (US-based reports) require consenting to federal court jurisdiction and accepting service of process.
- (ev_006) Designated agent is Etsy, Inc., Legal Department, 117 Adams Street, Brooklyn, NY 11201; page last updated Jun 16, 2023.

## Limitations
- The page explicitly disclaims being legal advice and notes it "may be updated periodically"; counter-notice procedures are stated to apply only to US-based copyright infringement reports.
