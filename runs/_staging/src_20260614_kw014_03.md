# Source Card: A standard platform fee for new creators — effective after August 4, 2025 — Patreon Help Center

## Summary
This official Patreon Help Center article establishes the platform's standard 10% platform fee for creators who publish their creator page after August 4, 2025, while creators with pages published on or before that date keep their existing legacy pricing. It documents the broader 5%-12% platform-fee range across plans, the separate per-transaction payment processing rate (2.9% + $0.30 for USD credit card / Apple Pay), the features included in the 10% fee, and a 2.5% currency conversion fee. It is authoritative for current Patreon creator monetization economics.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) Standard 10% platform fee applies to creators publishing after August 4, 2025.
- (ev_002) Creators publishing on or before August 4, 2025 retain existing (legacy) pricing with no change.
- (ev_003) Unpublishing a legacy page forces a switch to standard 10% pricing on republish.
- (ev_004) Platform fee ranges 5%-12% of processed sales + applicable taxes, depending on plan.
- (ev_005) USD payment processing rate is 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (credit card / Apple Pay, payments > $3).
- (ev_006) The 10% fee includes all core features (hosted page, memberships, digital sales, video hosting, community tools, insights).
- (ev_007) A 2.5% currency conversion fee applies for cross-currency payments.

## Limitations
- The article references the 5%-12% range and "existing platform pricing" for legacy creators but does NOT itemize the specific Lite 5% / Pro 8% / Premium 12% legacy tiers, nor does it use "consolidated Pro and Premium" framing; those points from the source brief are not verbatim-citable from this page and were intentionally omitted.
- The page displays a relative "Updated 4 months ago" timestamp rather than an absolute revision date; published_at (2025-08) is approximated from the August 4, 2025 effective date.
