# Source Card: Generate Audio Overview in NotebookLM

## Summary
This official Google NotebookLM Help Center article documents how to generate, manage, share, and download Audio Overviews from uploaded sources. It establishes the available output formats (Deep Dive, The Brief, The Critique, The Debate), 80+ language support, edit-access requirements, sharing/download options, and interactive-mode constraints. The article does not cover Studio export of Notes/Reports/Slide Decks, source-file export, Google Takeout, or per-source-type ingestion limits.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) Audio Overviews are exported via a Download control in the audio player; the article does not name a file format.
- (ev_002) Four selectable Audio Overview formats: Deep Dive (default), The Brief, The Critique, The Debate.
- (ev_003) Audio Overviews can be generated in 80+ languages, defaulting to the Google Account preference.
- (ev_004) Generating or deleting an Audio Overview requires edit access to the notebook.
- (ev_005) Public sharing of Audio Overviews is consumer-only and disabled for Workspace Enterprise/Education accounts.
- (ev_006) Interactive mode is English-only; voice and transcribed interactions are not stored or shared.
- (ev_007) AI-generated voices may contain inaccuracies or glitches; generation can take a couple of minutes.
