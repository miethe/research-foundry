# Source Card: OpenAI (X/Twitter): deep research reports now exportable as well-formatted PDFs

## Summary
On 2025-05-12, OpenAI's official X account announced that ChatGPT Deep Research reports can be exported as well-formatted PDFs that preserve tables, images, linked (clickable) citations, and a sources list. The export is triggered from the share icon via a "Download as PDF" option and applies to both new and past reports. Secondary press coverage reports availability for ChatGPT Plus, Team, and Pro tiers.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) PDF export preserves tables, images, linked citations, and sources.
- (ev_002) Export path: click the share icon, then select "Download as PDF."
- (ev_003) Works for both new and past deep research reports.
- (ev_004) Announced 2025-05-12; reported available to Plus, Team, and Pro users (secondary).

## Limitations
- The x.com page is authentication-gated (direct fetch returned HTTP 402); the verbatim tweet quote was obtained from the search index's rendering of the tweet text. The user-tier breakdown (Plus/Team/Pro) and the Deep Research launch date (2025-02-02) come from secondary press coverage, not from the tweet itself.
