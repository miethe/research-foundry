# Source Card: Compare plans that include generative AI | Adobe Firefly

## Summary
Adobe's official Firefly plans page defines a four-tier, credit-metered pricing model for generative AI: Standard (US$9.99/mo, 2,000 credits), Pro (US$19.99/mo, 4,000 credits), Pro Plus (US$49.99/mo, 10,000 credits), and Premium (US$199.99/mo, 50,000 credits). Generative credits are a fixed monthly allocation, and specific operation types (e.g., video generation, sound effects, audio/video translation) carry their own per-tier caps — a concrete example of metering by operation cost. When credits are exhausted, Adobe points users to paid credit add-on plans.

## Key evidence
- (ev_001) Firefly Pro Plus is US$49.99/mo regular (US$34.97/mo first-year promo).
- (ev_002) Firefly Premium is US$199.99/mo regular (US$139.91/mo first-year promo).
- (ev_003) Monthly generative-credit allocations scale by tier: 2,000 / 4,000 / 10,000 / 50,000.
- (ev_004) Entry tiers: Standard US$9.99/mo, Pro US$19.99/mo.
- (ev_005) Per-operation metering: 5-second video generations capped at 20/40/100 by tier.
- (ev_006) Out-of-credit path is paid credit add-on plans, not an on-page reduced-speed guarantee.

## Limitations
- The live page has been updated since the 2026 source snapshot (now four tiers) and reflects a limited-time promotion ending Aug 26; regular prices are the durable figures.
- The FAQ answer "What happens when I run out of generative credits" is a collapsed accordion and was not captured; the source metadata's "reduced speed / graceful degradation" claim could NOT be verified from the visible page text and is therefore not asserted here.
- "Unlimited generations" benefits are advertised as first-year promotional, not permanent plan entitlements. US pricing only.
